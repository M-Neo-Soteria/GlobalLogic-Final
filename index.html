⚠️ Important Data Storage Information

Local Storage Only: All data entered in this application is stored locally in your browser's storage.

Browser Cache Reset: If you clear your browser's cache or cookies, or switch to a different browser/device, the data will be lost.

Data Backup: To preserve your data permanently, you can export your checklist entries to CSV or PDF format directly from any checklist page and save it to your machine.

📋 Available Features

  • Store checklist data locally in your browser
  • Export data to CSV format for spreadsheet applications
  • Export data to PDF format for printing and archival
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Access your checklists anytime, offline support
